*Project: FORMATIONS - Phase 2 - Issued by Technical Director*
*GREEN LIGHT: proceed immediately upon reading. Escalate only per governance triggers.*

## Context
WO-016b's root-cause note is correct in diagnosis and honest in reporting, and its
adopted fix satisfied the TD directive as literally worded. But the TD directive
was wrong: it demanded a NUMBER (closing speed 12-15 m/s) instead of the right
MODEL. charge_speed_si_scale = 3.375 multiplies Impact/reporting only while the
block still moves at 4.0 m/s — a charge stat wearing a physics costume, which R4
rejects.

Real cavalry trots (~3.5–4.0 m/s — our sim's tactical meter) and gallops
(~13.5 m/s) only in the final run-up. Implement that gait; delete the conversion.
